Dong Anh Quoc is an undergraduate student in the Department of Mechatronics at the School of Mechanical Engineering, Hanoi University of Science and Technology, and a member of the AiRA Laboratory.

His research topics include brain–computer interfaces (BCI), EEG signal processing, and robotics applications that leverage neural decoding for intelligent control.
